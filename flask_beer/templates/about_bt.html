  • How does beer taster work? (current)
Beer taster is a way to find beers that have similar flavors to those you already love. By using natural language processing it allows the user to enter in flavors or characteristics that they're looking for, or not looking for, and get back a list of the most similar beers according to their description. This approach helps users both find flavors they like in other beer styles, while also letting them find specific flavors within a style.
